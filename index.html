Instructions for Google Chrome users

  1. If your bookmarks bar is not visible (the block beneith the address bar) press Ctrl+Shift+B to show it.
  2. Drag the "Make It Worse" button to your bookmarks bar. This will save it as a link.
  3. Visit any website, pour yourself a glass of Umbongo and click the link in your bookmarks bar to see how wonderful it could have been if it had been made 16 years ago.
